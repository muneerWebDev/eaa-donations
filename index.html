Increase access to education for out-of-school children through the self-learning program for Syrian refugees and IDPs

ABOUT

Al Fakhoora launched Dynamic Futures Gaza, awarding comprehensive scholarship packages for marginalized youth, enabling them to pursue undergraduate studies while also equipping them to be articulate, impassioned self-advocates.

  • A total of 971 scholarships have been awarded
  • 937 Undergraduate scholarships
  • 34 Master’s degree scholarships
  • All scholarship recipients are accessing a range of student support services
  • To date, 627 students have been trained on leadership and advocacy skills.
